The Story

Fabric as a
first language

KOTO began in a Cairo workshop with a single question: what does restraint look like when sewn into cloth? We source single-origin Egyptian long-staple cotton — the same fiber that dressed pharaohs — and cut it into pieces designed to outlast every trend.

Every seam is hand-finished. Every pattern is drawn once and never repeated beyond its limited run. We make less, on purpose.

"Luxury is not what you add.
It is what you refuse to remove."
